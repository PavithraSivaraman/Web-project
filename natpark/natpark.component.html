National Parks of India

1. Ranthambore National Park

Located in Rajasthan, it is known for its Bengal tiger population.

2. Kaziranga National Park

Famous for the Indian one-horned rhinoceros, located in Assam.

3. Jim Corbett National Park

Located in Uttarakhand, it is the oldest national park in India.

4. Sundarbans National Park

Famous for its mangrove forests and Royal Bengal tigers, located in West Bengal.

5. Nagarhole National Park

Located in Karnataka, known for its wildlife and scenic beauty.

6. Periyar National Park

Located in Kerala, famous for its elephants and scenic lake.

7. Keoladeo National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Rajasthan, known for birdwatching.

8. Bandhavgarh National Park

Located in Madhya Pradesh, it has one of the highest densities of tigers in the world.

9. Manas National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Assam, known for its biodiversity.

10. Gir National Park

Located in Gujarat, it is the only natural habitat of the Asiatic lion.
